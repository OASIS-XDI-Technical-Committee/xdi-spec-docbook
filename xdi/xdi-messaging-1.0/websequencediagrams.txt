title XDI Messaging Terminology

Requesting Peer (A)->Responding Peer (B): Request Message (M1)
note right of Requesting Peer (A)
    A is the Sending Peer
    B is the Receiving Peer
end note
Responding Peer (B)->Requesting Peer (A): Result Graph or Response Message (M2)
note right of Requesting Peer (A)
    B is the Sending Peer
    A is the Receiving Peer
end note
